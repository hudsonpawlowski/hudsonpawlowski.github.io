About

Bringing energy, heart, and showmanship to every performance.

Hudson is an emerging actor and musical theatre performer with a strong love for live storytelling, character work, and the excitement of stepping into a number or scene with full commitment. At 16, he is focused on training, gaining experience, and developing the kind of versatility that musical theatre demands, from acting choices to stage presence and ensemble awareness. This site is a professional home base for the materials and credits that will grow with his work.

Current Focus

Acting technique, vocal performance, rehearsal discipline, and growing as a musical theatre artist.

Performance Style

Bright energy, emotional honesty, collaborative spirit, and a clear love for being onstage.
